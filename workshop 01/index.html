Lyn Park

Interviewed by Ariel

What music do you like to listen to while working? - typically watches shows when working, but if listening to music will listen to jazz like chet baker

Name three designers whose work you find inspiring.

1. Wolfgang Tilmans - likes the niche quality of his work and the uniqueness of his displays, even worked with famous artists like Frank Ocean

2. James M. Jetter - head designer for Ralph Lauren, likes sleek elegance and old money style fashion

3. Karel Martens - Graphic designer, plays a lot with colour and shapes

Name something (a format, an item) you want to learn how to design but haven't had the chance to try to yet. - Sewing and making clothes or even tailoring clothes

If you were a font, you'd be (you can't say helvetica or comic sans-- both jokes are played). Explain why? - Letter Gothic

Name one website that you rely on (try to avoid a platform like google/amazon/pinterest/etc). - Naver (Korean Google)

If you were an animal what would you be? - otter
